A jugaad for everything

At DoJugaad we intend to unite all the jugaad and jugaadu people of a world at one place and share their awesome ability of jugaad with others. We are open and welcome for problems, jugaadu ideas and jugaads of all sorts - from paintings to accessories, from technology and science to some random day-to-day problem.In short, we aim at making this world an easier place to live. We aim, to find a jugaad for every problem, together.
