At DoJugaad we intend to unite all the jugaad and jugaadu people of the world at one place and share their awesome ability of jugaad with others. We heartily welcome problems, jugaadu ideas and jugaads of all sorts - from paintings to accessories, from technology and science to some random day-to-day problem.In short, we aim at making this world an easier place to live. We aim, to find a jugaad for every problem, together.

 
Have a problem ?

Share

Problems that you share on DoJugaad, are made open for numerous jugaadu people out there. They brainstorm on the problem, suggest ideas, make improvements to the ideas and finally come up with an optimal solution. From there, we take over to make a product of that optimal idea.

Have an idea ?

Let us know

We, as mentioned above, bring your ideas to life so that they are no longer confined to the walls of your brain. Nor they are limited by any constraints. In short you need not worry about how to make a product of it.
