Q.

What is DoJugaad?

A.

DoJugaad is a platform where people can seek some cool and creative solutions (the jugaad) to their monotonous day-to-day problems. It is also a place where innovative people can have their innovation (jugaad) converted to an awesome market ready product. This is not it, DoJugaad also enables user to sell off their product and make money. At DoJugaad, people are also welcome to influence the process of making of products.

Q.

How can I contribute to DoJugaad?

A.

There are various ways by which you can contribute:
1. Come up with a problem and seek an innovative solution to the problem.
2. Come up with an innovative idea to solve some existing problem; in a better way. The ideas will be then filtered to get a few best ideas for manufacture.
3. Come up with an innovative product. You can submit the product and let us provide you a place where you can sell your products.
4. In case you do not have any of the above, you can still contribute to the design and manufacture of the ideas.

Q.

How am I benefited?

A.

You get your share of work from the revenue generated by the product. You can make a contribution to DoJugaad and earn a certain share of revenue. The exact share is to be decided on per product basis.

Q.

How it works?

A.

Customer Involvement:DoJugaad works simply on share and earn policy. We engage customer interest by providing portal for submission of ideas, problems or a product as whole. Customers can also be a part of our design and manufacturing process.
Product selling and Profit:Here, at DoJugaad, we make each contribution count. The design ideas or products submitted by you can be sold to the interested buyer. A customer will be given profit on his/her contribution subject to our profit policies. Contributions can be made as described in FAQ Q2.

Q.

What kind of ideas can I submit?

A.

Well, we believe in innovation. Any kind of idea that solely belongs to you and that can be transformed in a product is welcome with open arms.

Q.

What kind of problem can I submit?

A.

We treat each problem equally. For us, each problem is a challenge whether it's a daily life small problem or a corporate level massive problem. we aim to solve any kind of problem that can make even slight difference in your life.

Q.

What kind of product can you submit?

A.

Here at DoJugaad, you are free to submit any kind of product that can be effectively used to resolve the problems of people.
In short, any product that you think can be a jugaad to a problem,can be submitted here.

Q.

Is it free to make submission?

A.

Yes,it is absolutely free to make any kind of submission.However, considering various constraint ,we may need to filter submissions based on our filtering process.
